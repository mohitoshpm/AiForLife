Parking Automation & Management System

Finding a safe and secure parking lot in an over populated city like Dhaka is a deepest concern for vehicle users. The proposed project aims to develop a prototype of an automated car park management system that will increase the quality of parking service, utilizing the advancement of Internet of things (IoT) that assists motorists both finding parking location and fair charge. The project has encompassed both implementation of an automatic location tracking, parking management, monitoring, real time bill generation and payment system. The overall system enables an efficient Parking Guidance and mutual information system concept for parking providers and motorists. The consequences of the project include but not limited to the safety of the vehicles, valuables inside the vehicle and boldly reduction of traffic jam from the city. Deep neural network based character recognition was employed to count and put into list all the cars entering the parking area. All the parking activities in this system are monitorable by “LCD Display & Web Application”. The Automated Car Park Management System was successfully built by utilizing microcontrollers & microprocessor specifically “Arduino Uno R3 (Microcontroller) &Raspberry Pi 3 Model B (Microprocessor)”.
